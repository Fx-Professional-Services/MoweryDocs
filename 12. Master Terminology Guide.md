## 📂 General Terms

|Term / Abbreviation|Definition|
|---|---|
|**ACH**|Automated Clearing House. An electronic network for bank-to-bank payments.|
|**Transaction ID**|A unique reference number for a payment (e.g., check number, electronic ID).|
|**Balance**|The amount owed or credited on a customer’s account.|
|**Property**|A customer location (e.g., home, building) where services are performed.|

---

## 📄 Service Agreements Terms

|Term / Abbreviation|Definition|
|---|---|
|**SA**|Service Agreement. A contract outlining ongoing services for a customer.|
|**Installment Letter**|A letter outlining payment schedules for a Service Agreement.|
|**Renewal Proposal**|A document generated to renew an existing Service Agreement.|
|**Agreement Details**|Section within a Service Agreement record where actions (like payments) are managed.|

---

## 🛠️ Service Calls Terms

|Term / Abbreviation|Definition|
|---|---|
|**Dispatch**|Assigning a technician to a service call.|
|**Schedule Board**|The visual board where unassigned and scheduled calls are managed.|
|**Parts List**|A list of materials required for a specific service call.|
|**COD**|Cash on Delivery. A customer designation requiring payment at service time.|

---

## 👥 Customer Management Terms

|Term / Abbreviation|Definition|
|---|---|
|**MCP**|Master Customer Property list. A centralized view of customer properties.|
|**Detail View**|The screen showing full customer information and property details.|
|**Category**|A classification for a service or product (e.g., HVAC, Plumbing).|
|**Subcategory**|A further breakdown within a category (required for certain products).|

---

## 🖨️ Printing and Messaging Terms

|Term / Abbreviation|Definition|
|---|---|
|**Send to Parts**|Function that generates and sends a parts request directly from a Service Call. The request is logged in the system for tracking.|
|**Default Parts Printer**|The system-selected printer used when printing parts lists.|
|**Automated Message**|A system-generated message triggered by customer or call conditions (e.g., COD).|
|**Twilio**|The messaging service integrated with Mowery to send customer notifications.|
