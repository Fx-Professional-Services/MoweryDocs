
# Sales Report Page

This guide explains how to use the **Sales Report** page to generate and review sales data.

---

## Overview

The **Sales Report** page allows you to:

- Specify a reporting period
    
- Generate a sales report
    
- Preview the report before exporting or printing
    

Use this page to analyze revenue and sales activity within a selected date range.

---

## Page Layout

The page contains three main sections:

1. **Report section** (top left)
    
2. **Criteria panel** (left side)
    
3. **Preview panel** (right side)
    

### Report Section

- **Report field** – Displays or accepts the report name.
    

### Criteria Panel

Use this panel to define the date range for the report.

Fields:

- **Date Range** – Select a predefined range (if available).
    
- **Start Date** – Enter the beginning date of the reporting period.
    
- **End Date** – Enter the ending date of the reporting period.
    

Buttons:

- **Run Report** – Generates the report using the selected criteria.
    
- **Cancel** – Clears changes or exits without generating a report.
    

### Preview Panel

Displays the generated report.  
The preview updates after you click **Run Report**.

---

## Generate a Sales Report

Follow these steps to generate a report:

1. Open the **Sales Report** page.
    
2. In the **Criteria** panel:
    
    - Select a **Date Range**,  
        or
        
    - Enter a **Start Date** and **End Date**.
        
3. Click **Run Report**.
    

The report appears in the **Preview** panel.

---

## Set a Custom Date Range

To use specific dates:

1. Leave the **Date Range** field blank (if applicable).
    
2. Enter a **Start Date**.
    
3. Enter an **End Date**.
    
4. Click **Run Report**.
    

Ensure the **End Date** is later than or equal to the **Start Date**.

---

## Cancel a Report

To cancel:

1. Click **Cancel**.
    
2. The system clears any unsaved selections.
    

---

## Best Practices

- Verify the date range before running the report.
    
- Use consistent reporting periods (for example, monthly or quarterly) for accurate comparisons.
    
- Confirm that the preview displays expected data before sharing or exporting.
    

---

## Troubleshooting

### The report does not generate

- Ensure both **Start Date** and **End Date** are entered.
    
- Confirm that the date format is correct.
    
- Verify that the selected date range contains sales data.
    

### The preview is blank

- Check that the selected date range includes transactions.
    
- Re-run the report after adjusting the dates.
