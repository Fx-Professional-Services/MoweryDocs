# Service Agreement

## Service Agreement Status

The system displays customer equipment based on the status of the customer’s **Service Agreement**. This ensures technicians and dispatchers see only relevant equipment information.

## How Equipment Is Displayed

The equipment list updates automatically according to the customer’s service agreement status. Use the following table as a reference:

|Service Agreement Status|Equipment Display|Notes / Labels|
|---|---|---|
|**Active**|Show all associated equipment|No additional labels|
|**Expired**|Show associated equipment|Label: **Expired** + expiration date|
|**None**|No equipment displayed|Equipment section is hidden|
