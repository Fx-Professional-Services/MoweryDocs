# Toggleable Mode for Call Creation 

The **Toggleable Mode for Call Creation** controls whether technicians can create new service calls. Dispatchers always retain full call-creation access.

---

## Roles

### Technician

A technician is a field worker who completes service tasks at customer locations. Technicians normally receive and update service calls through the mobile app. When Call Creation Mode is enabled, technicians can also create new calls (Night Mode).

### Dispatcher

A dispatcher assigns service calls to technicians and manages scheduling. Dispatchers can always create, edit, and assign calls.

---

## Feature Overview

The toggle determines whether technicians see the **Create Call** option in the mobile app.

- **ON**: Technicians can create new service calls.
    
- **OFF**: Technicians can view and update their assigned calls only.
    

Dispatchers are not affected by the toggle.

---

## Technician Call Creation (When Enabled)

When Call Creation Mode is ON, technicians can:

- Create a new service call.
    
- Enter customer information and describe the issue.
    
- Submit the call for dispatcher review.
    

Use this mode for:

- After-hours emergencies
    
- Weekend or overnight on-call shifts
    
- Regions without real-time dispatcher coverage
    

---

## Dispatcher Workflow

Dispatchers:

- Always create and edit service calls.
    
- Assign technicians to calls.
    
- Review calls created by technicians during Night Mode.
    

When a technician submits a call, the dispatcher:

1. Reviews the details.
    
2. Confirms customer information.
    
3. Assigns the call to the appropriate technician.
    

---

## How the Toggle Works

An administrator or system setting enables or disables technician call creation.  
When the toggle changes:

- Technician permissions update immediately.
    
- The mobile app shows or hides the **Create Call** option.
