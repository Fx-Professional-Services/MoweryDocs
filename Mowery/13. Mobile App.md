## Sign and Send the Invoice

After generating the invoice, sign and email it to the customer.

## Steps

1. Tap **Generate Invoice**.
    
2. Enter the **Technician Name** and **Customer Name**.
    
3. Add the **Technician Signature** and **Customer Signature** in the fields below the invoice.
    
4. After signing, tap **Email PDF**.
    
    - This generates the signed invoice as a PDF and sends it to the customer.
        
5. When prompted, enter the recipient’s email address.
    
6. Tap **Send**.
    
    - If the email address is missing or invalid, an error message appears.
        
    - If the email is valid, a notification confirms that the signed invoice was sent successfully.
