# Send an Estimate After Signing

Use this procedure to sign and send an estimate to the customer.

## Steps

1. Complete the estimate.
    
2. Tap **Sign Estimate**.
    
3. Add the signature in the **“I AUTHORIZE REPAIRS TO BE DONE”** section.
    
4. When prompted, enter the recipient’s email address.
    
5. Tap **OK**.
    

The system sends the signed estimate to the specified email address.
