## Create a Collection Report for Payment Received

Use this procedure to generate a collection report for payments received by a technician.

## Steps

1. From the **Main Menu**, click **Payments**.
    
2. Select a payment record to view its **Payment Details**.
    
3. On the left side, select a **Technician** from the **dropdown list**.
    
4. Click **Collection Report**.
    
5. Review the list of payment records. You can click on the following columns to fill in details:
    
    - **Received** – enter the amount received.
        
    - **Date Received** – enter the date the payment was recorded.
        
    - **Received By** – enter the technician who received the payment.
