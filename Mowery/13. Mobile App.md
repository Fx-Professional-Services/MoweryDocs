# Technician Guide: Close Out Button 

## Overview

Use **Close Out** to finish a completed service call in the mobile app. The app creates a communication record from the invoice and saves it to the customer file. This gives dispatch immediate access to the final job details.

Use **Close Out** only after you complete the job and finish the invoice.

---

## Before You Start

Make sure all of the following are complete:

- Service call is finished
- Invoice is created
- Technician signature is captured
- Customer signature is captured

If required information is missing, the app may block submission.

---

## Open the Close Out Screen

1. Open the service call.
2. Tap **Invoice**.
3. Tap **Close Out**.

The **Close Out** screen opens.

---

## Complete the Form

## Type

Select one option:

- **Close Out** – Complete the service call
- **Add Work** – Add more completed work
- **Parts Order** – Order parts before final completion

For finished calls, select **Close Out**.

---

## Review Existing Fields

The app may prefill these fields:

- **Invoice Number**
- **Invoice Date**
- **Call Date**
- **Technician Name**

Check each field for accuracy.

---

## Call Time

Enter the time the call was completed.

---

## Description

Enter a short summary of the work completed.

Include useful details such as:

- Problem found
- Repairs made
- Parts replaced
- System status
- Follow-up recommendations

Example:

Replaced capacitor. Tested system. Cooling normally.

---

## Service Agreement

Select one option if applicable:

- **Annual**
- **Monthly**

---

## Billing

Enter the amount billed, if required by your process.

---

## Collected

Enter the amount collected from the customer.

If no payment was collected, leave blank or follow company policy.

---

## Payment Type

If you collected payment, select a payment type:

- Cash
- Check

---

## Check Number

If the customer paid by check, enter the check number.

---

## Submit or Cancel

### Submit

Tap **Submit** to:

- Save the close out record
- Update dispatch
- Add the communication record to the customer file
- Complete the service call

### Cancel

Tap **Cancel** to leave the screen without saving.

---

## Example

Customer paid $325 by check.

1. Select **Close Out**.
2. Review invoice details.
3. Enter work summary.
4. Enter **Collected: 325**.
5. Select **Check**.
6. Enter the check number.
7. Tap **Submit**.
