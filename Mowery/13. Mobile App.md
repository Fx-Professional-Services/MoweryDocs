# Email Signed Estimate to Customer

Use this procedure to sign and send an estimate to the customer.

1. Complete the estimate.
    
2. Tap **Sign Estimate**.
    
3. Add the signature in the **“I AUTHORIZE REPAIRS TO BE DONE”** section.
    
4. After signing, tap **Email PDF**.
    
    - This generates the signed estimate as a PDF and sends it to the customer.
        
5. When prompted, enter the recipient’s email address.
    
6. Tap **OK**.
    
    - If the email address is missing or invalid, an error message appears.
        
7. A notification confirms that the signed estimate was sent successfully.
