# Monthly Tune Up Schedule

## Tune Up Schedule and Tune Up Calendar

The **Tune Up Schedule** and **Tune Up Calendar** work together to show tune-up availability in the system.

---
### Tune Up Schedule

- Located in the **bottom-right corner** of the main FileMaker screen.
    
- Sets the **number of tune-ups allowed per day**.
    

---
### Tune Up Calendar

1. Open the **HVAC board**.
    
2. Open any **scheduled service call**.
    
3. The **Tune Up Calendar** appears on the **left side** of the screen.
    

---

### Current Behavior

- The Tune Up Calendar **matches the schedule visually**.
    
- The **daily tune-up limit** set in the Tune Up Schedule is **not enforced** in the Tune Up Calendar.
    

> **Important:** The Tune Up Calendar shows scheduled tune-ups but does not stop users from exceeding the allowed number set in the Tune Up Schedule.
