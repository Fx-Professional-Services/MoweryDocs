## View the Monthly Tune-Up Schedule

Use the Monthly Tune-Up Schedule to see upcoming and completed tune-up dates.

## Steps

1. From the **Service Management System** screen, locate the **Monthly Tune-Up Schedule** in the lower-right corner.
    
2. Click **Monthly Tune-Up Schedule**.
    

A list of dates appears on the left side of the screen.

## How the Schedule Works

- The **current day** appears at the top of the list.
    
- **Completed days** are automatically removed from the list.
    
- Only upcoming and current dates remain visible.
