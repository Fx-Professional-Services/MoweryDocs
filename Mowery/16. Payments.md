
# Payments

## Overview

The **Payment** screen allows staff to track customer payments and manage the payment status. You can search for customers, view payment methods, and mark payments as **Collected**, **Received**, or **Deposited**.

Use this page to ensure all payments move through the correct processing stages.

---

## Open the Payment screen

1. From the main menu, click **Payments**.
2. The Payment page opens and displays a list of customer payment records.

---

## Payment screen layout

The Payment screen contains the following elements:

### Search field

Use the **Search** field at the top of the page to filter payment records.

Example searches:

- Customer name
    
- Partial customer name
    

The results update to show matching records.

---

### Payment table

The payment table displays payment records in rows.

|Column|Description|
|---|---|
|**Customer**|The name of the customer who made the payment.|
|**Payment Method**|The type of payment used (for example: Cash or Check).|
|**Collected**|Indicates the payment has been collected from the customer.|
|**Received**|Indicates the payment has been received by the business.|
|**Deposited**|Indicates the payment has been deposited into the bank.|

Each payment record appears on its own row.

---

## Update a payment status

Follow these steps to update a payment status.

1. Locate the payment in the table.
    
2. Check the appropriate box in one of the following columns:
    
    - **Collected**
        
    - **Received**
        
    - **Deposited**
        
3. The system saves the update automatically.
    

### Example

If a cash payment from **Gary Ogle** has been collected but not yet deposited:

1. Find the row with **Gary Ogle**.
    
2. Check the **Collected** box.
    
3. Leave **Received** and **Deposited** unchecked until those steps occur.
    
---

## Search for a customer payment

1. Click inside the **Search** field.
    
2. Enter the customer's name.
    
3. Press **Enter**.
    

The table filters to show matching payment records.

To clear the search, delete the text from the Search field.

---

## Best practices

- Update payment status as soon as each step occurs.
    
- Verify the payment method before marking a payment as received.
    
- Mark payments as **Deposited** only after the bank deposit is completed.
    

---

### Incorrect payment status

If a status was checked by mistake:

1. Uncheck the box.
    
2. Select the correct status.
