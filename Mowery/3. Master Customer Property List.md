# Master Customer Property List

The **Master Customer Property List** (also called the *Customer Property Card List*) is a searchable table of all customers. Service staff, dispatchers, and office administrators use this layout to access contact information, service agreements, and equipment records.

## Available actions

From the Master Customer Property List, you can:

- Search for a customer by name, phone number, or address  
- View service status, contact numbers, and equipment counts  
- Open the [Detail View](#customer-detail-view) to access full customer records  
- Filter or sort customers by ZIP code, city, or customer type  
- Check if a property has service agreements, proposals, or pending orders  
- Print or review customer records for scheduling, billing, or dispatch

## Column descriptions

The **Customer Property Card List** displays the following columns.

|Column|Description|
|---|---|
|**Customer Type**|Indicates whether the property is **Residential** or **Commercial**.|
|**Customer / Email**|Displays the customer’s name and email address.|
|**Bill To Property / City / ZIP**|Shows the billing address, including city and ZIP code.|
|**Phone Numbers**|Lists all phone numbers associated with the account.|
|**Phone Descriptions**|Identifies the owner of each phone number (for example, _Husband_, _Wife_, _Jessica_).|
|**# SAs**|Shows the number of service agreements linked to the property.|
|**# PAs**|Shows the number of proposals created for the customer.|
|**# Equip.**|Shows the number of equipment items recorded for the property.|
|**Service Agreement Status**|Displays the status of the service agreement: **Active**, **Expired**, or **Missing**.|

---
### Toolbar actions

At the top of the layout, you can:

- Click **New Customer** to add a new record  
- Use **Find** or **Find All** to search for or list all customers  
- Use **Sort** to group customers by city, ZIP code, or name  
- Use **QuickFind** to search by phone number (area code required)

## Customer Detail View

The **Customer Detail View** shows all information related to a customer and their property. Open this view by clicking the **Detail View icon** **(blue arrow)** in the [Master Customer Property List](#master-customer-property-list).

### Available actions

In the Customer Detail View, you can:

- Edit customer contact and billing details  
- View and update property information  
- Add or review equipment, service agreements, and service call history  
- Record directions, notes, or communication logs  
- Add new service records

#### COD Only (Cash on Delivery)

The **COD Only** feature ensures customers are informed about payment requirements before services are provided.

##### Mark a customer as COD

1. Open the [Master Customer Property List](#master-customer-property-list).
    
2. Click the **Detail View icon** (blue arrow) to open the customer record.
    
3. In the upper-right of the **Customer Information** panel, check the **COD** box.

  <img src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/Fx-Professional-Services/MoweryDocs/staging/Mowery/Mowery%20User%20Guide/Images/cod_customer.png" 
       alt="COD Only (Cash on Delivery)" style="width: 75%;" />
</p>


Once marked, COD alerts appear when you create a [Service Call](https://github.com/Fx-Professional-Services/MoweryDocs/blob/staging/Mowery/4.%20Service%20Calls.md).

##### Service call for a COD customer

1. From the main menu, go to [Service Calls](https://github.com/Fx-Professional-Services/MoweryDocs/blob/staging/Mowery/4.%20Service%20Calls.md).
    
2. Search for the customer by name.
    
3. Select the customer (highlighted in red) from the search results.
    
4. Click **Start New Service Call**, then click **Create a Call**.
    
5. When prompted:
    
    - Click **Yes** to confirm creating the call.
        
    - A second dialog appears: _"Chip says: Sell a service agreement."_  
        → Click **OK**.
        
6. Once the service call is scheduled:
    
    - **Status** updates to `Scheduled`.
        
    - **Description** displays:  
        _**COD**_ in red text for visibility.
       
##### Customize the COD message

1. From the main menu, go to [System Defaults](https://github.com/Fx-Professional-Services/MoweryDocs/blob/staging/Mowery/6.%20System%20Defaults.md).
    
2. Click **Developer Settings**.
    
3. At the bottom-right, find the **COD Message** field.
    
4. Enter or modify the COD instructions that appear during service calls.

Example: _“This customer is COD. Collect payment at the time of service.”_

### Upload Documents to Property Cards

The **Document Upload** feature lets you attach files directly to a Property Card. This allows you to store and access important property-related documents—all in one place.

---
##### Overview

With this feature, you can:

- Upload files to a property card
    
- Add a short description for each file
    
- View thumbnail previews for supported file types (PDF, JPG, PNG)
    
- Export and open attachments using your computer’s default program
    
- Delete entries when no longer needed
    

---
##### Steps

To upload and manage documents:

1. From the main menu, go to **Master Customer Property List**.
    
2. Select a property from the list and click the **right arrow** on the left to open the **Details View**.
    
3. Click the **Document** tab.
    
4. In the **Description** field, enter a short note about the file.
    
5. Click the 📎 (clip icon) to attach a document.
    
6. To delete an entry, click the ❌ (X icon) next to the document.
    
7. To view a document, click its thumbnail.
    
    - The document opens in a **new window** without prompting to save the file.

## Lock Commercial Customer Property Cards

This feature restricts editing of **commercial customer property cards** to authorized employees only. It protects business information and prevents accidental changes to commercial accounts.

##### Overview

- **Purpose:** Prevent unauthorized edits to commercial property fields.
    
- **Scope:** Applies only to **Commercial** customers. Residential customer records are unaffected.
    
- **Controlled Fields:** Business Name, Billing Name, Customer Type, and other commercial-only fields.

---
##### Who Can Edit Commercial Fields

Editing access is controlled via a checkbox in the employee’s FileMaker profile:

**Permission:** `Can modify commercial records at property card`

| Checkbox State | Employee Access                                                                              |
| -------------- | -------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Checked (X)    | Employee can edit commercial property fields                                                 |
| Unchecked      | Employee cannot edit commercial property fields; attempts are blocked with a warning message |

**Designated administrators** can enable or disable this permission:

- Autumn Tyra
    
- Val
    
- Jay
    
- Andy
    

---
##### How to Enable Permission

1. From the main menu, go to **System Defaults**.
    
2. In the upper-right corner, click **FileMaker Access**.
    
3. Click the blue box in the upper-left to select an employee from the list.
    
4. Select the checkbox: **Can Modify Commercial Records at Property Card**.
    

Employees with this permission can now edit locked commercial fields.

## Audit trail

At the bottom of the screen:

- **Created By** – Name of the user who created the record.  
- **Modified By** – Last user who updated the record and the timestamp.

---
## Property Cards Cannot Be Deleted

Property cards cannot be deleted from the system. The delete option is disabled in the user interface and is not available in Manage Property Cards. All property cards remain stored for reporting and service call history.

## Terminology Guide

This glossary explains key terms used in the **Customer Management Guide**. Each entry links to the section where you can learn more.  

* **Audit Trail** – A log showing who created or last modified a customer record, with timestamps.
→ See [Audit trail](#audit-trail).  

* **COD (Cash on Delivery)**
A payment status that requires the customer to pay before service is provided. COD customers are flagged in red in the system.  
→ See [COD Only](#cod-only-cash-on-delivery).  

* **Customer Record**
Also called the *Customer Property Card* or *Detail View*. Stores all customer information, property details, equipment, agreements, and service history.  
→ See [Customer Detail View](#customer-detail-view).  

* **Customer Type** 
Classification of a customer as either **Residential** or **Commercial**.  
→ See [Customer information](#customer-information).  

* **Detail View Icon (Blue Arrow)**  
The icon in the Master Customer Property List used to open the full customer record.  
→ See [Customer Detail View](#customer-detail-view).  

 * **Equipment**  
HVAC or plumbing items installed at a customer’s property. Equipment is tracked for maintenance, service calls, and agreements.  
→ See [Tab navigation](#tab-navigation).  

* **Master Customer Property List**  
A searchable table of all customers in the system. Staff use this layout to view, filter, and open customer records.  
→ See [Master Customer Property List](#master-customer-property-list).  

* **Property Information**  
Details about the service location, including address, directions, property type, and notes.  
→ See [Property information](#property-information-step-2-section).  

* **Service Agreement (SA)**  
A contract for ongoing service at a customer’s property. Service agreements may be **Active** or **Expired**.  
→ See [Tab navigation](#tab-navigation).  

* **Service Call**  
A scheduled job such as installation, repair, or maintenance. Service calls are linked to customer records and agreements.  
→ See [Service Calls](https://github.com/Fx-Professional-Services/MoweryDocs/blob/staging/Mowery/4.%20Service%20Calls.md).  

* **System Defaults**
The admin configuration area for managing system-wide settings, including COD messages, catalogs, and developer options.  
→ See [System Defaults](https://github.com/Fx-Professional-Services/MoweryDocs/blob/staging/Mowery/6.%20System%20Defaults.md).  

* **Toolbar Actions**
The set of actions at the top of the Master Customer Property List, such as **New Customer**, **Find**, **Sort**, and **QuickFind**.  
→ See [Toolbar actions](#toolbar-actions).
