# Service Calls User Guide

## Overview

The **Service Calls** module lets you manage all customer service jobs.  
Jobs may include installation, repair, maintenance, or inspection for HVAC, plumbing, or other services.

Use this section to schedule jobs, assign technicians, communicate with customers, and track service history.

## Purpose

A **Service Call** is a scheduled visit to a customer’s property for a specific service task.  
Each call tracks job details, technician assignments, communications, and parts requests in one place.
___
## Key Features

With the **Service Calls** module, you can:

- Schedule and manage jobs
    
- Create and track service call records
    
- Assign and dispatch technicians
    
- Notify customers with job details by text message
    
- View and manage the SMS Inbox on the Dispatch Board
    
- Print service tickets and parts lists
    
- Record communications and call history
    
- Manage staff and technician records
    
- Organize products by category

## Assign a Technician Using the Dispatch Board

Use the **HVAC Schedule Board** to assign available technicians to unassigned service calls.

### To assign a technician:

1. From the main menu, select **Service Calls**.  
    The **Schedule Board** opens. Unassigned calls appear in the **Unassigned** column.
    
2. Select a call to open the **Service Call Detail** screen.  
    Or click **List View** (upper left) to view all calls.

  <img src="https://github.com/Fx-Professional-Services/MoweryDocs/blob/e222dbdf97bf98fc4f6b7d7e6988d30a856c3900/Mowery/Mowery%20User%20Guide/Images/list_view_assign_technician.png" 
       alt="List View" style="width: 75%;" />
</p>

*Click **List View** to display all service calls in a list format.*

3. In the **Status** column, select **Scheduled Service Calls**, then click the **Expand Arrow** to view call details.
    
<p style="float: right; margin-left: 15px; width: 20%;">
  <img src="https://github.com/Fx-Professional-Services/MoweryDocs/blob/e222dbdf97bf98fc4f6b7d7e6988d30a856c3900/Mowery/Mowery%20User%20Guide/Images/arrow_list_view.png" 
       alt="Expand Arrow" style="width: 75%;" />

*Click the arrow to expand call details in the Status column.*

4. Under **Step 2: Assign Technician**, click **New**.
    
5. In the **Technician** field, select a technician from the dropdown.
    
6. Click **Confirm** (blue arrow) to record the dispatch time.

*Click **Confirm** to log the technician assignment.*

7. If two technicians are assigned, confirm both before sending a text.
    
8.  Click **Dispatch** (green arrow) to update the status and log the time.

*Click the **Blue Phone Icon** to send a text with technician details.*

## Send and Manage Text Message Confirmations

Use text message confirmations to coordinate with customers before dispatching a technician.  
Customers can confirm appointments directly through a secure link.

---

## Step 1: Send the Appointment Confirmation Text

### Step 1: Send an Appointment Confirmation Text

1. From the main menu, go to **Service Calls**.
    
2. Open the scheduled service call record.
    
3. In **Step 2: Assign Technician**, verify technician availability but do **not** assign yet.
    
4. In the **Contact** section, select **Text**.
    
5. Ensure the customer’s phone number:
    
    - Is entered in the **Master Property List**
        
    - Has **Accept Text Message** checked
        
    - Is a valid 10-digit U.S. number
        
6. Click the **Blue Phone Icon**.
    
7. Click **Send Message** to send the confirmation.
    

**Example confirmation message:**

Hi, this is Mowery Heating, Cooling, and Plumbing confirming your appointment for today.
Your technician is now available. Is now a good time for you?
Click here to confirm your appointment: [Insert Link]
Thank you for choosing Mowery. We look forward to seeing you soon!


---
### Step 2: Customer Confirmation

When the customer opens the link, a secure webpage appears showing:

- Appointment schedule
    
- Service type
    
- Job description
    
- Confirmation prompt with **Yes** or **No** buttons
    

**If the customer clicks Yes:**

- The system logs the confirmation date and time.
    
- The message _“The service call is already confirmed.”_ appears.
    
- The service call record updates to show **Appointment Confirmed**.
    

**If the customer clicks No:**

- The appointment remains unconfirmed.
    
- Dispatchers can reassign or reschedule as needed.
    
      
    _Customer confirmation page showing appointment details and Yes/No buttons._
    

---  
### Step 3: Send Technician Details

After receiving a **Yes** reply:

1. Click the **Blue Phone Icon** again.
    
2. Send the technician introduction message, which includes:
    
    - Appointment date and time
        
    - Technician name and photo
        
    - Link to view job details
        
    
      
    _Example technician introduction message sent after customer confirmation._
    
---
### Step 4: View and Manage Replies in SMS Inbox

1. From the main menu, go to **Service Calls → SMS Inbox**.
    
2. View customer replies in real time.
    
3. Send follow-up messages directly from the inbox.

### Best Practices for Dispatchers

- Respond promptly—aim to reply within a few minutes.
    
- Keep messages short and professional.
    
- Confirm appointment details, such as times and technician names.
    
- Escalate or flag messages that require follow-up.
    
- Maintain a polite and courteous tone at all times.**

- Customers receive a link to the **Technician Profile Page**.
    
- Clicking the link confirms the appointment and logs the timestamp.
    
- Links are secure, unique, and expire when the call is closed.
### What Customers See

- Service status (Scheduled, In Progress, Completed)
    
- Appointment time
    
- Technician name and photo
    
### Security

- Secure link (no login required)
    
- Customer sees only their assigned technician

## Send a Parts List from a Service Call

Dispatchers can email a parts request directly from a service call record.  
This automatically notifies the parts department and logs the request.

### To send a parts list:

1. From the main menu, open **Service Calls**.
    
2. Select a service call record.
    
3. Scroll to **Step 4** and click **Send to Parts**.


<p style="float: right; margin-left: 15px; width: 20%;">
  <img src="https://github.com/Fx-Professional-Services/MoweryDocs/blob/staging/Mowery/Mowery%20User%20Guide/Images/send_to_parts.png" 
       alt="Send to Parts" 
       style="width: 60%;" />
</p>

*Click **Send to Parts** to email the parts request to the appropriate department.*

When clicked, the system:

- Sends an email to `hvac-parts@makeitmowery.com` or `plumbing-parts@makeitmowery.com`.
    
- Logs your name and the timestamp in the service call record.

## Search for Customers by Phone Number

You can search for customers using a full or partial phone number.

### From Service Calls

1. From the main menu, go to **Service Calls**.
    
2. Click **List View**.
    
3. In the search field, enter a customer’s name, number, or address.
    
    - Example: `3178527770`, `(317) 852-7770`, or `317-852-7770`.
        
4. Press **Return** (Mac) or **Enter** (Windows).

> **Note:** Phone numbers must include the area code and follow the format `xxx-xxx-xxxx`.

2. Enter the customer’s name, phone number, or address.
    
3. Press **Return** or **Enter** to search.
    
4. Verify that the correct record appears.

## Dispatch Board Date Sync Update

The **Dispatch Board** now stays synchronized across all boards (HVAC, Plumbing, Estimates, etc.) when changing dates.

### How It Works

1. Open any dispatch board (e.g., HVAC).
    
2. Select a new date.
    
3. Switch to another board (e.g., Plumbing or Estimates).  
    The same date now appears automatically.

*All dispatch boards now display the schedule for the selected date automatically.*

# Update Customer Names in Property Cards

Use this procedure to update a customer’s **Company/Last Name** or **First Name** from **Property Cards**.

## Steps

1. From the **Dashboard**, open **Service Calls**.
    
2. Select **Property Cards** from the top menu.
    
3. Select a property from the list.
    
4. Click the **left arrow** to open **Property Details**.
    
    The **Property Details** view shows:
    
    - **Step 1: Billing Address**
        
    - **Step 2: Property Address**
        
5. Update the **Company/Last Name** and/or **First Name** fields.
    

## Notes

- Updating the customer name does **not** change:
    
    - **Billing Address (Step 1)**
        
    - **Property Address (Step 2)**
