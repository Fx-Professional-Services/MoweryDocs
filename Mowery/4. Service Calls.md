# Service Calls User Guide

## Overview

The **Service Calls** module lets you manage all customer service jobs.  
Jobs may include installation, repair, maintenance, or inspection for HVAC, plumbing, or other services.

Use this section to schedule jobs, assign technicians, communicate with customers, and track service history.

## Purpose

A **Service Call** is a scheduled visit to a customer’s property for a specific service task.  
Each call tracks job details, technician assignments, communications, and parts requests in one place.
___
## Key Features

With the **Service Calls** module, you can:

- Schedule and manage jobs
    
- Create and track service call records
    
- Assign and dispatch technicians
    
- Notify customers with job details by text message
    
- View and manage the SMS Inbox on the Dispatch Board
    
- Print service tickets and parts lists
    
- Record communications and call history
    
- Manage staff and technician records
    
- Organize products by category

## Remove a Technician from a Service Call

### Overview

Remove a technician from a service call when they are no longer assigned or were added by mistake.

### Steps

1. Open **Service Call** from the main menu.
2. Click **List View**.
3. Select the service call, then click the arrow on the left to open it.
4. Go to **Step 2: Technician Time**.
5. Click the **Delete (X)** icon next to the technician’s name.
6. In the confirmation prompt, click **Yes**.

### Result

The technician is removed from the service call.

## Send and Manage Text Message Confirmations

Use text message confirmations to coordinate with customers before dispatching a technician.  
Customers can confirm appointments directly through a secure link.

---
### Step 1: Send an Appointment Confirmation Text

1. From the main menu, click **Service Calls**.
    
2. Click **List View**.
    
3. Select the scheduled service call.
    
4. Click the left arrow to open the service call record.
    
5. In **Step 2: Technician Time**, verify technician availability.  
    Do **not** assign the technician yet.
    
6. In the **Contact Method** section, select **Text**.
    
7. Click the **Blue Phone** icon.
    
8. Click **Send Message**.
    

The system sends the confirmation text message to the customer.

**Example confirmation message:**

>Hello___, this is Mowery Heating, Cooling, and Plumbing confirming your appointment for today.  
  Your technician is now available. Is now a good time for you?

Click here to confirm your appointment: **[Insert Link]**

Thank you for choosing Mowery. We look forward to seeing you soon!

---
### Step 2: Customer Confirmation

When the customer opens the link, a secure webpage appears showing:

- Appointment schedule
    
- Service type
    
- Job description
    
- Confirmation prompt with **Yes** or **No** buttons
    

**If the customer clicks Yes:**

- **When the customer replies YES:**

- The response appears in **SERVICE CALLS > SMS Inbox**.
- The SMS Inbox shows conversations grouped **per customer** and **per service call**.
- Open the thread to view the entire message history between the dispatcher and the customer.
- The dispatcher can reply directly inside that same message thread.

**After the YES confirmation:**

- The system logs the confirmation date and time.
    
- The message _“The service call is already confirmed.”_ appears.
    
- The **Service Call** record updates to show **Appointment Confirmed** above the **Contact Information** section.
    

**If the customer clicks No:**

- The appointment remains unconfirmed.
    
- Dispatchers can reassign or reschedule as needed.

# Parts Request System User Guide

## Overview

The **Parts Request System** allows technicians to request parts quickly after completing a service call.  
Each request automatically includes the customer and job information, helping the parts team process it without delay.

This feature ensures that missing or replacement parts are properly tracked, ordered, and fulfilled.

---
## When to Use

Use the **Parts Request** feature whenever you complete a service call and discover that a part needs to be ordered, replaced, or restocked.

---

## Opening the Parts Request Form

1. In **Mowery Oct**, open the **Service Calls** menu.
    
2. Go to the **Dispatch Board** and select the completed service call.
    
3. Click **Parts Request**.
    
4. When prompted with **“Do you need to request a part?”**:
    
    - Click **Yes** to open the Parts Request Form.
        
    - Click **Cancel** to return to the service call.
        

---

## Filling Out the Parts Request Form

The form automatically includes customer and service call details.  
Enter only the specific information about the part being requested.

|Field|Description|
|---|---|
|**Description**|Brief description of the required part.|
|**Quantity**|Number of units needed.|
|**Model Number**|Model number of the part.|
|**Serial Number**|Serial number, if applicable.|
|**Urgency Level**|Indicates how soon the part is needed (optional).|
|**Date Requested**|Automatically recorded when the form is submitted.|
|**Status**|Displays progress: _Open_, _Fulfilled_, or _Canceled_.|

---

## Adding Notes

Use notes to provide additional details that help clarify your request.

1. In the Parts Request window, open the **Part Selector** dropdown.
    
2. Select the part you want to comment on.
    
3. Click **New Note**.
    
4. Enter your note in the text field.
    
5. To add another note, click **New** again or select a different part.
    

**Examples of helpful notes:**

- Installation location or special instructions
    
- Compatibility details
    
- Urgency explanations (e.g., “Customer has no heat—part needed ASAP”)
    

---

## Example Workflow

1. Complete your service call.
    
2. When prompted, click **Yes** to request a part.
    
3. Review the pre-filled customer information.
    
4. Enter details for the required part (description, quantity, model, serial).
    
5. Add notes if necessary.
    
6. Submit the form.
    

Once submitted, the request is automatically saved and visible to the parts team for review and fulfillment.

---

## Parts Request Management View

The **Parts Request Management View** allows parts staff to monitor, review, and filter all submitted parts requests in one place.  
This view ensures timely tracking, organization, and coordination of all part orders.

### Overview

The screen displays key details for each request, helping staff track progress and manage fulfillment efficiently.

**Displayed Fields:**

- **Request ID** – Unique identifier for each request
    
- **Date Requested** – When the request was submitted
    
- **Customer Name** – Linked to the related service call
    
- **Technician Name** – Who submitted the request
    
- **Status** – Indicates whether the request is _Open_ or _Fulfilled_
    

**Available Filters:**

- **Department** – Filter by service type (e.g., HVAC or Plumbing)
    
- **Status** – View only _Open_ or _Fulfilled_ requests
    
- **Date Range** – Narrow results to a specific time period
    

---

## Tracking Requests

After submission, each request appears in the system with a **status** showing its current stage:

- **Open** – Waiting to be processed.
    
- **Fulfilled** – The part has been ordered or received.
    
- **Canceled** – The request was withdrawn or no longer needed.
    

Technicians and office staff can monitor these statuses to track progress and follow up when needed.

---

## Tips

- Always double-check the **Model Number** and **Quantity** before submitting.
    
- Use the **Urgency Level** field for time-sensitive parts.
    
- Add concise, specific notes to help the parts team locate the correct item.
    
- If a request was submitted in error, contact the parts department to have it updated or canceled.

# Parts Request System

## Overview

The **Parts Request** system allows technicians to log parts needed immediately after completing a service call.  
Each request is linked to a **customer**, **service call**, and **property address**, ensuring the parts team has all the details required to take action.

This system is the first phase of the improved parts ordering and tracking process.

---

## Goal

Provide technicians with a simple, standardized method to submit parts requests so that requests can be efficiently **tracked**, **grouped**, and **fulfilled**.

---

## Access the Parts Request System

Follow these steps to open the **Parts Request** feature:

1. Open **Mowery Oct**.
    
2. From the main menu, select **Service Calls**.  
    ![Service Calls menu](../images/parts-request/image-service-calls.png)
    
3. Choose a service call from the **Dispatch Board**.  
    ![Dispatch Board](../images/parts-request/image-dispatch-board.png)
    
4. Click **Parts Request**.  
    ![Parts Request button](../images/parts-request/image-parts-request-button.png)
    

A confirmation prompt appears:

- Click **Yes** to create a new parts request.
    
- Click **Cancel** to return to the service call.  
    ![Parts Request prompt](../images/parts-request/image-parts-request-prompt.png)
    

---

## Create a Parts Request

After confirming, a new **Parts Request** record opens.  
In the **Details** section, fill in the required fields:

|Field|Description|
|---|---|
|**Model Number**|Enter the model of the required part.|
|**Quantity**|Specify how many parts are needed.|
|**Description**|Provide a short description of the part.|
|**Date**|Enter the date of the request.|
|**Status**|Select the current status (for example, _Pending_, _Ordered_, _Received_).|

![Parts Request details section](../images/parts-request/image-parts-request-details.png)

---

## Add Notes to a Parts Request

You can add notes to provide more context about specific parts.

1. In the **Parts Request** window, open the **Part Selector** dropdown.  
    ![Part selector dropdown](../images/parts-request/image-part-selector.png)
    
2. Select the part you want to add a note to.
    
3. Click **New Note**.
    
4. Enter your note in the text field.
    
5. To add another note, either:
    
    - Click **New**, or
        
    - Use the **Part Selector** dropdown to select another part and repeat the process.  
        ![Adding a note](../images/parts-request/image-new-note.png)
        

---

## Example Workflow

1. Complete a service call.
    
2. Open **Parts Request** from the Service Call record.
    
3. Enter the required part details in the **Details** section.
    
4. Add notes as needed for specific parts.
    
5. Submit the request for review and processing by the parts team.
    

![Parts Request workflow summary](../images/parts-request/image-parts-request-workflow.png)

---

## Parts Request Information

Each parts request contains important details that help the parts team identify and fulfill the needed items.  
The system automatically links the request to the technician, customer, and service call.

|Information|Description|
|---|---|
|**Technician Name**|Identifies who submitted the request.|
|**Customer Name**|Shows which customer the request is for.|
|**Service Call**|Links the request to the related service call record.|
|**Part Description**|Describes the part being requested.|
|**Model Number**|Lists the model of the part, if known.|
|**Serial Number**|Records the serial number, if applicable.|
|**Date Requested**|Shows when the request was made.|
|**Urgency Level**|(Optional) Indicates if the request is urgent.|
|**Status**|Displays whether the request is _Open_, _Fulfilled_, or _Canceled_.|

This information ensures each request can be tracked, updated, and fulfilled accurately.
