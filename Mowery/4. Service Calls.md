# Service Calls

The **Service Calls** section is where you manage all service jobs. These jobs may include installation, repair, maintenance, or inspection for HVAC, plumbing, or other customer services.

</p>

### Purpose

A **Service Call** is a scheduled visit to a customer’s property for a specific service task.

### Key Features

With Service Calls, you can:

* Schedule and manage jobs
* Create and track service calls
* Assign and dispatch technicians
* Notify customers with job details
* Use the SMS Inbox on the Dispatch Board to send, receive, and manage text messages
* Print service tickets and parts lists
* Record communications and history
* Manage staff and technician records
* Organize products by category

## Assign Technicians Using the Dispatch Board

The **HVAC Schedule Board** lets dispatchers quicly assign technicians to unassigned [Service Calls](#terminology-reference) quickly.

To assign a technician from the **Schedule Board**:

1. From the main menu, select **Service Calls**.
2. The **Schedule Board** opens. Unassigned [HVAC](#terminology-reference) calls appear in the **Unassigned** column.
3. Select a call to open the **Service Call Detail** screen.  
   - Or click **List View** (upper left).

       alt="List View" style="width: 75%;" />

- In the **Status** column, select **Scheduled Service Calls**, then click the arrow.

       alt="Expand Arrow" style="width: 75%;" />

4. In **Step 2: Assign Technician**, click **New**.
    
5. In the **Technician** field, select a technician from the dropdown.

# Send and Manage Text Message Confirmations

This feature allows dispatchers to confirm appointments by text message and receive customer replies directly in FileMaker.  
Once the customer confirms they are ready, the dispatcher assigns the technician and sends the technician introduction message.

---

## Step 1: Send the Appointment Confirmation Text

1. From the main menu, go to **Service Calls**.
    
2. Open the scheduled **Service Call** record.
    
3. In **Step 2: Assign Technician**:
    
    - Verify that the technician is **available**, but do **not assign them yet**.
        
4. In the **Contact** section:
    
    - Select **Text**.
        
    - Verify that the customer’s phone number is:
        
        - Entered in the **Master Property List**
            
        - Checked as **Accept Text Message**
            
        - A valid **10-digit U.S. number**
            
5. Click the **Blue Phone Icon**.
    
6. The system opens the text message box. Click **Send Message** to send the confirmation text to the customer.
    

**Message sent to customer:**

>Hello___, this is Mowery Heating, Cooling, and Plumbing confirming your appointment for today.  
  Your technician is now available. Is now a good time for you?

Click here to confirm your appointment: **[Insert Link]**

Thank you for choosing Mowery. We look forward to seeing you soon!

---

## Step 2: Customer Replies to the Confirmation

1. After the dispatcher sends the confirmation message, the customer receives a **text message** asking them to confirm the appointment.
2. The customer replies **YES** to confirm the appointment.

---

### System Behavior

- **When the customer replies YES:**

- The response appears in **SERVICE CALLS > SMS Inbox**.
- The SMS Inbox shows conversations grouped **per customer** and **per service call**.
- Open the thread to view the entire message history between the dispatcher and the customer.
- The dispatcher can reply directly inside that same message thread.

- **After the YES confirmation:**

- The system logs the date and time of the confirmation.
- The **Service Call** record updates to show **Appointment Confirmed** above the **Contact Information** section.

---

## Step 3: Send the Technician Details Message

The technician message confirms that the job is being dispatched and includes a link to view **Service Details**.

**Service Details include:**

- **Status:** Scheduled
    
- **Appointment Date and Time**
    
- **Technician Information:** Name and Photo
    

---

## Step 4: View and Manage Replies in SMS Inbox

1. From the main menu, go to **Service Calls → SMS Inbox**.
    
2. View customer replies and send follow-up messages directly.
    
3. The **SMS Inbox** remains visible across layouts (e.g., Dispatch Board), allowing dispatchers to monitor messages in real time.

# SMS Inbox on Dispatch Board

The **SMS Inbox** provides dispatchers with a real-time view of incoming text messages. It allows you to quickly read, organize, and reply to messages without leaving the **Dispatch Board**.

The SMS box now remains visible even when navigating to other screens in FileMaker. This ensures dispatchers can see customer replies in real time, regardless of which call or layout is open.

---

## Open the SMS Inbox

1. From the main menu, go to **Service Calls**.
    
2. Click **SMS Inbox**.
    
3. The inbox displays recent inbound messages and stays visible across screens.
    

<p style="float: right; margin-left: 15px; width: 20%;"> <img src="https://github.com/Fx-Professional-Services/MoweryDocs/blob/id_1364/Mowery/Mowery%20User%20Guide/Images/1364_sms_inbox_overview.png" alt="SMS Inbox Overview" style="width: 75%;" /> </p>

---

## View a Conversation

1. In the **SMS Inbox**, select a message from the list.
    
2. The full message thread opens within the persistent SMS box.
    
3. Scroll through the thread to review the conversation history while continuing to work on other tasks.
    

---

## Reply to a Message

1. Open a conversation in the **SMS Inbox**.
    
2. Enter your reply in the message field.
    
3. Click **Send**.
    
4. Your message appears at the bottom of the thread. The SMS box remains open for continued visibility.
    

<p style="float: right; margin-left: 15px; width: 20%;"> <img src="https://github.com/Fx-Professional-Services/MoweryDocs/blob/id_1364/Mowery/Mowery%20User%20Guide/Images/1364_sms_reply_sent.png" alt="SMS Reply Sent" style="width: 75%;" /> </p>

## Best Practices for Dispatchers

- **Respond promptly.** Aim to reply within a few minutes.
    
- **Keep responses clear and professional.** Use short, direct sentences.
    
- **Confirm details.** Repeat appointment times, technician names, or arrival windows.
    
- **Escalate when needed.** Flag any messages that require follow-up.

## Send a Parts List from a Service Call

Dispatchers can email a parts request directly from the Service Call screen. This notifies the parts department quickly and records the request digitally.

### Send a Parts List from a Service Calls

1. From the main menu, open **Service Calls**.
    
2. Select a record from the right column.
    
3. Scroll to **Step 4**.
    
4. Click **Send to Parts**.
    

When clicked, the system:

- Sends an email to **hvac-parts@makeitmowery.com** or **plumbing-parts@makeitmowery.com**
    
- Logs your name and timestamp in the Service Call record

## View and Manage Staff Records

### View the Staff List

1. In **Service Calls**, select **Staff.** 
2. The **Staff List** shows all employees (read-only).
### Edit a Record

1. In **Staff List**, click **Form View** (number to the left of the employee’s name).  
2. The **FileMaker Access** layout opens.  
3. Edit details (auto-save).

1. In **FileMaker Access**, click **New**.

## 📞 Search for Customers by Phone Number

You can search for customers using a **full or partial phone number**. 

### Search from Service Calls

1. From the main menu, go to **Service Calls**.
    
2. Click the **List View** button (left side of the screen).
    
3. In the search field above the list, enter a customer’s **name, number, or address**.
    
    - Example: Enter `3178527770`, `(317) 852-7770`, or `317-852-7770` to find the same record.
    - Phone numbers must include the area code and follow the format: `xxx-xxx-xxxx`
        
4. Press **Return** (Mac) or **Enter** (Windows), or **Tab**.
    
5. Review the results to confirm that the correct customer records appear.

### 🗂️ Search from the Customer Property Card

1. In the results list, click the **left arrow button** in the leftmost area of a row to open the **Customer Property Card** screen.
    
2. In the search field at the top, enter the same name, phone number, or address.
* Phone numbers must include the area code and follow the format: xxx-xxx-xxxx. 
    
1. Press **Return** (Mac) or **Enter** (Windows) or **Tab**.
    
2. Verify that the correct customer records are displayed.

## Terminology Reference
| Term                    | Description                                                                 |
| ----------------------- | --------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Service Call            | A scheduled visit for installation, repair, maintenance, or inspection work |
| HVAC                    | Heating, Ventilation, and Air Conditioning                                  |
| Send to Parts           | Function that emails a job-specific parts list to the parts department      |
| Send to Parts field     | Record showing who sent the request and when                                |
