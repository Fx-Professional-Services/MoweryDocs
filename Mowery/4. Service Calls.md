# Service Calls User Guide

## Overview

The **Service Calls** module lets you manage all customer service jobs.  
Jobs may include installation, repair, maintenance, or inspection for HVAC, plumbing, or other services.

Use this section to schedule jobs, assign technicians, communicate with customers, and track service history.

## Purpose

A **Service Call** is a scheduled visit to a customer’s property for a specific service task.  
Each call tracks job details, technician assignments, communications, and parts requests in one place.
___
## Key Features

With the **Service Calls** module, you can:

- Schedule and manage jobs
    
- Create and track service call records
    
- Assign and dispatch technicians
    
- Notify customers with job details by text message
    
- View and manage the SMS Inbox on the Dispatch Board
    
- Print service tickets and parts lists
    
- Record communications and call history
    
- Manage staff and technician records
    
- Organize products by category

## Send a Text Message with Technician Details

You can send customers a personalized text message with technician and appointment details, but only after certain conditions are met.

### 🔒 Requirements

- The service call **must be confirmed**.  
  - In **Step 2: Assign Technician**, the dispatcher must click the **blue arrow** and enter the time under **Confirm**.  
- A technician must be **assigned to the service call**.  
  - If no technician is assigned, clicking the **Send Text Message** icon will result in a pop-up:  
    **“No Technician selected. Please assign a technician before sending a message.”**
- The customer's **phone number** must be:  
  - Entered on the **Master Property List**  
  - Marked as **Preferred**  
  - Checked with **Accept Text Message**  
  - A valid **10-digit U.S. format**

### ✅ How to Send a Text Message

1. From the main menu, go to **Service Calls**.  
2. Open the scheduled and confirmed **service call**.  
3. In **Step 2: Assign Technician**:  
   - Verify a technician is assigned  
   - Confirm the call by entering the confirmation time via the blue arrow  
4. In the **Contact** section:  
   4.1. Ensure **Text** is selected  
   4.2. Phone number is valid and marked **Preferred**  
   4.3. **Accept Text Message** is checked  
5. Click the **Send Text Message** (blue phone icon).  
6. Wait for the confirmation or error message.

## Send and Manage Text Message Confirmations

Use text message confirmations to coordinate with customers before dispatching a technician.

---

## Step 1: Send the Appointment Confirmation Text

### Step 1: Send an Appointment Confirmation Text

1. From the main menu, go to **Service Calls**.
    
2. Open the scheduled service call record.
    
3. In **Step 2: Assign Technician**, verify technician availability but do **not** assign yet.
    
4. In the **Contact** section, select **Text**.
    
5. Ensure the customer’s phone number:
    
    - Is entered in the **Master Property List**
        
    - Has **Accept Text Message** checked
        
    - Is a valid 10-digit U.S. number
        
6. Click the **Blue Phone Icon**.
    
7. Click **Send Message** to send the confirmation.
    

**Example confirmation message:**

Hi, this is Mowery Heating, Cooling, and Plumbing confirming your appointment for today.
Your technician is now available. Is now a good time for you?
Click here to confirm your appointment: [Insert Link]
Thank you for choosing Mowery. We look forward to seeing you soon!


---
### Step 2: Customer Confirmation

When the customer opens the link, a secure webpage appears showing:

- Appointment schedule
    
- Service type
    
- Job description
    
- Confirmation prompt with **Yes** or **No** buttons
    

**If the customer clicks Yes:**

- The system logs the confirmation date and time.
    
- The message _“The service call is already confirmed.”_ appears.
    
- The service call record updates to show **Appointment Confirmed**.
    

**If the customer clicks No:**

- The appointment remains unconfirmed.
    
- Dispatchers can reassign or reschedule as needed.
    
      
    _Customer confirmation page showing appointment details and Yes/No buttons._
    

---  
### Step 3: Send Technician Details

After receiving a **Yes** reply:

1. Click the **Blue Phone Icon** again.
    
2. Send the technician introduction message, which includes:
    
    - Appointment date and time
        
    - Technician name and photo
        
    - Link to view job details
        
    
      
    _Example technician introduction message sent after customer confirmation._

### Step 4: View and Manage Replies in SMS Inbox

## 🖨️ **Print a Parts List from a Service Call**

You can generate a printed parts list directly from a Service Call record using the **Send to Parts** feature. This streamlines dispatch operations and provides a record of who printed the parts list and when.

#### ✅ **What Happens When You Click Send to Parts**

When a dispatcher clicks the **Send to Parts** button:

- The system automatically updates the Send to Parts** field with:

- The name of the dispatcher (account name).
- The date and time the button was clicked.

📌 This field acts as a log to help track print activity for accountability.

#### 🔄 **Steps to Use Send to Parts**

1. From the **main menu**, go to **Service Call**.
2. Click the **List View** button in the upper left corner.
3. Select a completed call by clicking the **right arrow icon** next to it.  
    This opens the **Customer Property Card —Service Call Data Entry** screen.
4. Scroll to the bottom-right of the screen (Step 4 section).
5. Click the **Send to Parts** button:

- One of the following will happen:

- The call details are printed automatically if your printer is set to FM_PartTicket.
- A print dialog appears, allowing you to select or set up a printer.

7. After clicking the button:

- The **Send to Parts** field (bottom left) updates with:

- Your account name.
- The timestamp of the print action.

<img src="https://github.com/Fx-Professional-Services/MoweryDocs/blob/id_1275/Mowery/Mowery%20User%20Guide/Images/send_to_parts_button.png" width="350" height="230">

#### 🛠️ **Default Printer Behavior**

When you click **Send to Parts**, the system sends the job to the default printer (FM_PartTicket), if configured.

You don’t need to manually select a printer unless no default printer is set.

✅ **Benefits**

- 🕒 Saves time
- ✅ Reduces user errors
- 🚀 Improves dispatch efficiency

## Search for Customers by Phone Number

You can search for customers using a full or partial phone number.

### From Service Calls

1. From the main menu, go to **Service Calls**.
    
2. Click **List View**.
    
3. In the search field, enter a customer’s name, number, or address.
    
    - Example: `3178527770`, `(317) 852-7770`, or `317-852-7770`.
        
4. Press **Return** (Mac) or **Enter** (Windows).

> **Note:** Phone numbers must include the area code and follow the format `xxx-xxx-xxxx`.

## Dispatch Board Date Sync Update

The **Dispatch Board** now stays synchronized across all boards (HVAC, Plumbing, Estimates, etc.) when changing dates.

### How It Works

1. Open any dispatch board (e.g., HVAC).
    
2. Select a new date.
    
3. Switch to another board (e.g., Plumbing or Estimates).  
    The same date now appears automatically.

*All dispatch boards now display the schedule for the selected date automatically.*

# Property Notes

Use **Property Notes** to add or update information that appears on both **Service Calls** and **Property Cards**.

## Update Property Notes

1. From the main menu, select **Service Calls**.
    
2. In the upper-left corner, click **List View**.
    
3. Click the **right arrow** to select a service call from the list.
    
4. In the **Service Setup** section, add or edit **Property Notes**.
    
5. To format the text:
    
    - Highlight the text.
        
    - Hold **Control** (macOS) or **Ctrl** (Windows), then right-click.
        
    - Choose the formatting options you want.
        
6. Changes are saved automatically and applied to the associated **Property Card**.
    

## Verify the Updates

1. Select **Property Cards** from the navigation tabs.
    
2. Click the **right arrow** to open the property.
    
3. Confirm that the **Property Notes** match the updates made in **Service Calls**.
