# Service Calls

The **Service Calls** section is where you manage all service jobs. These jobs may include installation, repair, maintenance, or inspection for HVAC, plumbing, or other customer services.

### Purpose

A **Service Call** is a scheduled visit to a customer’s property for a specific service task.

### Key Features

With Service Calls, you can:

* Schedule and manage jobs
* Create and track service calls
* Assign and dispatch technicians
* Notify customers with job details
* Use the SMS Inbox on the Dispatch Board to send, receive, and manage text messages
* Print service tickets and parts lists
* Record communications and history
* Manage staff and technician records
* Organize products by category

## Assign Technicians Using the Dispatch Board

The **HVAC Schedule Board** lets dispatchers quicly assign technicians to unassigned [Service Calls](#terminology-reference) quickly.

To assign a technician from the **Schedule Board**:

1. From the main menu, select **Service Calls**.
2. The **Schedule Board** opens. Unassigned [HVAC](#terminology-reference) calls appear in the **Unassigned** column.
3. Select a call to open the **Service Call Detail** screen.  
   - Or click **List View** (upper left).

       alt="List View" style="width: 75%;" />

- In the **Status** column, select **Scheduled Service Calls**, then click the arrow.

       alt="Expand Arrow" style="width: 75%;" />

4. In **Step 2: Assign Technician**, click **New**.
    
5. In the **Technician** field, select a technician from the dropdown.
    
6. Click **Confirm** (blue arrow) to record the dispatch time.

7. Click **Dispatch** (green arrow) to update the status to _Dispatch_ and log the time.

## Send a Text Message with Technician Details

You can send customers a text with technician and appointment details once requirements are met.

### Requirements

- The [Service Call](#terminology-reference) must be **confirmed**.
    
- A technician must be **assigned**.
    
    - If not, the message reads: _“No Technician selected. Please assign a technician before sending a message.”_
    
- The customer’s phone number must be:
    
    - Entered on the **Master Property List**
        
    - Marked as **Preferred**
        
    - Checked for **Accept Text Message**
        
    - A valid 10-digit U.S. number

### Send a Text Message

1. From the main menu, go to **Service Calls**.  
2. Open the scheduled and confirmed [**Service Call**](#terminology-reference).  
3. In **Step 2: Assign Technician**:  
   - Verify a technician is assigned  
   - Confirm the call by entering the confirmation time (blue arrow)  
4. In the **Contact** section:  
   - Select **Text**
   - Verify the number is valid, marked **Preferred**, and checked for **Accept Text Message**
5. Click **Send Text Message** (blue phone icon).  
6. Wait for the confirmation or error message.

<p style="float: right; margin-left: 15px; width: 20%;">
  <img src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/Fx-Professional-Services/MoweryDocs/staging/Mowery/Mowery%20User%20Guide/Images/send_text_message_technician_details.png" 
       alt="Send a Text Message with Technician Details" style="width: 75%;" />
</p>


### Message Contents 

The customer receives a message with:

- **Technician** name  
- **Customer address**  
- Link to the [**Technician Profile Page**](#terminology-reference)  
- Technician introduction  

**Example:**  
Hello [Customer Name], [Technician Name] from Mowery is on the way to [Customer Address].
Click here for more details: [Technician Profile Webpage Link]

Hi, my name is [Technician Name]. I’ll be your technician today. [Technician Bio] If you have any questions, I’m here to help. Thank you for choosing Mowery!

### Error Messages

- **No Technician selected** → Assign a technician first.
    
- **Preferred number missing** → Mark a number as preferred.
    
- **Accept Text checked but no number** → Enter a cell number.

## Best Practices for Dispatchers  

- **Respond promptly.** Aim to reply within a few minutes.  
- **Keep responses clear and professional.** Use short, direct sentences.  
- **Confirm details.** Repeat appointment times, technician names, or arrival windows.  
- **Escalate when needed.** Flag any messages that require follow-up.  
- **Maintain a courteous tone.** SMS messages represent the company’s customer service.

## Send a Parts List from a Service Call

Dispatchers can email a parts request directly from the Service Call screen. This notifies the parts department quickly and records the request digitally.

### Send a Parts List from a Service Calls

1. From the main menu, open **Service Calls**.
    
2. Select a record from the right column.
    
3. Scroll to **Step 4**.
    
4. Click **Send to Parts**.
    

When clicked, the system:

- Sends an email to **hvac-parts@makeitmowery.com** or **plumbing-parts@makeitmowery.com**
    
- Logs your name and timestamp in the Service Call record

## 📞 Search for Customers by Phone Number

You can search for customers using a **full or partial phone number**. 

### Search from Service Calls

1. From the main menu, go to **Service Calls**.
    
2. Click the **List View** button (left side of the screen).
    
3. In the search field above the list, enter a customer’s **name, number, or address**.
    
    - Example: Enter `3178527770`, `(317) 852-7770`, or `317-852-7770` to find the same record.
    - Phone numbers must include the area code and follow the format: `xxx-xxx-xxxx`
        
4. Press **Return** (Mac) or **Enter** (Windows), or **Tab**.
    
5. Review the results to confirm that the correct customer records appear.

# Dispatch Board Date Sync Update

The **Dispatch Board** has been updated to ensure that the displayed **date** and **schedule** always stay in sync when switching between different boards (HVAC, Plumbing, Estimates, etc.).

---

## Overview

Previously, when users changed the date on one dispatch board and switched to another, the **date field** sometimes displayed the new date while the **board content** still showed calls from the previous date.

This issue has been fixed. Now, all dispatch boards correctly display the schedule that matches the selected date—no manual refresh or reselecting the date is required.

---

## New Behavior

|Action|Previous Behavior|Updated Behavior|
|---|---|---|
|Change date on one board (e.g., Estimates)|Other boards retained the old schedule even if the date field changed|All boards now load the correct schedule for the selected date|
|Switch between boards (e.g., HVAC → Plumbing → Estimates)|The board sometimes displayed calls from the wrong date|Each board automatically refreshes and displays calls for the currently selected date|
|Manually reselect the date|Required to force the board to refresh|No longer necessary—boards auto-refresh correctly|

---

## How It Works Now

1. Go to any dispatch board (e.g., **HVAC**).
    
2. Select a new date from the date field.
    
3. Switch to another board, such as **Plumbing** or **Estimates**.
    
4. The new board automatically shows calls for the same date you selected—no extra steps required.
    

The date field and the board schedule are now always synchronized.
