
# 📄 Service Agreements

The **Service Agreements** section is used to create and manage long-term service contracts between Mowery and its customers. It helps ensure customers receive ongoing service while providing the company with predictable revenue.

### 🔍 Purpose

This area is used to create, edit, and maintain service agreements for properties.

### 🛠️ Key Features

- Create SA (Service Agreement) installment letters  
- Generate SA renewal proposals  
- Search for expired, active, or soon-to-expire service agreements  
- Print service agreements


# 💳 Accept Payment for Service Agreements

This guide explains how to record a payment for a Service Agreement in the Mowery system.

---

## 📍 Where to Find

To access the **Accept Payment** screen:

1. From the main menu, go to **Service Agreements**.
2. Select an existing service agreement from the list on the left.
3. Under the **Agreement Details** section, Step 2, click the **Accept Payment** button.

## 🧾 Fill Out the Payment Form

When the **Accept Payment** dialog appears, enter the following fields:

| Field            | Description                                                                                          |
|------------------|------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------|
| **Payment Type** | Enter the method of payment (e.g., Card, ACH).<br><br>**If Card** is selected, enter:<br>• Card number<br>• Expiration date<br>• CVV<br><br>**If ACH** is selected, enter:<br>• Account name<br>• Account number<br>• Routing number<br>• Account type |
| **Amount**       | Specify the exact amount received.                                                                   |
| **Description**  | (Optional) Add any notes about the transaction.                                                      |
| **Transaction ID** | Enter a reference number, such as a check number or electronic ID.                                 |

---

## ✅ Complete the Payment

- Click **Accept Payment** to save the transaction.
- Click **Cancel** to close the window without saving.

After clicking **Accept Payment**, a confirmation message will appear:  
**"This transaction has been approved."**  
Click **OK** to continue.

> ⚠️ Make sure the card used for the transaction is not expired. Otherwise, the transaction will be unsuccessful.

Once saved, the payment will be recorded in the customer's agreement history and reflected in their balance.
