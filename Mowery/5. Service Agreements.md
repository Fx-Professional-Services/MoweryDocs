# Service Agreements

The **Service Agreements** section allows you to create and manage long-term service contracts between Mowery and its customers.  
Service agreements ensure customers receive ongoing service while providing Mowery with predictable revenue.

---
## Purpose

Use this section to create, update, and manage service agreements for customer properties.

---

## Key Features

- Create **Service Agreement (SA)** installment letters
    
- Generate **renewal proposals** for existing agreements
    
- View **expired**, **active**, or **soon-to-expire** service agreements
    
- **Print** service agreements
    

---
# Accept Payment for Service Agreements

This guide explains how to record a payment for a service agreement.

1. From the main menu, select **Service Agreements**.
    
2. In the list on the left, choose the agreement you want to update.

	Note: Use the navigation tabs below to filter results in the Service Agreements list.
	Click Find Active to display active service agreements.

## Fill Out the Payment Form

In the **Accept Payment** window, enter the following details:

|Field|Description|
|---|---|
|**Payment Type**|Select the payment method (e.g., **Card**, **ACH**).  <br>  <br>**If Card:** Enter the card number, expiration date, and CVV.  <br>  <br>**If ACH:** Enter the account name, account number, routing number, and account type.|
|**Amount**|Enter the exact amount received.|
|**Description**|_(Optional)_ Add any notes about the payment.|
|**Transaction ID**|Enter a reference number, such as a check number or electronic payment ID.|

---

## Complete the Payment

1. Click **Accept Payment** to save the transaction.
    
2. Click **Cancel** to exit without saving.
    

After you click **Accept Payment**:

- A confirmation message appears:
    
    > **"This transaction has been approved."**
    
- Click **OK** to continue.
    

> ⚠️ **Important:** The transaction will fail if the card is expired.

Once approved, the payment is recorded in the customer’s agreement history and reflected in their balance.

## Terminology

|Term / Abbreviation|Definition|
|---|---|
|**SA**|_Service Agreement._ A contract for ongoing customer services.|
|**ACH**|_Automated Clearing House._ An electronic network for bank-to-bank payments.|
|**Transaction ID**|A unique reference number for a payment (e.g., check number or electronic ID).|
|**Agreement Details**|Section in a service agreement record where actions such as payments are managed.|
|**Installment Letter**|A letter showing the payment schedule for a service agreement.|
|**Renewal Proposal**|A document used to extend or renew a service agreement.|
