# Service Agreements

The **Service Agreements** section is used to create and manage long-term service contracts between Mowery and its customers. Service agreements ensure customers receive ongoing service while providing the company with predictable revenue.
### Purpose

Use this section to create, update, and manage service agreements for customer properties.

### Key Features

- Create SA (Service Agreement) installment letters  
- Make renewal proposals for existing agreements
- Find expired, active, or soon-to-expire service agreements  
- Print service agreements


# Accept Payment for Service Agreements

This guide show how to record a payment for a Service Agreement. 

## Access the Accept Payment Screen

1. From the main menu, go to **Service Agreements**.
    
2. Select the agreement you want from the list on the left.
    
3. In the **Agreement Details** section, Step 2, click **Accept Payment**.

## Fill Out the Payment Form

In the **Accept Payment** window, enter the following: 

| Field              | What to do                                                                                                                                                                                                |
| ------------------ | --------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Payment Type**   | Enter the method of payment (e.g., Card, ACH).<br><br>**If Card**: Enter card number, expiration date, and CVV. <br><br>**If ACH**: Enter account name, account number, routing number, and account type. |
| **Amount**         | Type the exact amount received.                                                                                                                                                                           |
| **Description**    | (Optional) Add any notes about the payment                                                                                                                                                                |
| **Transaction ID** | Enter a reference number, such as a check number or electronic ID.                                                                                                                                        |

## Complete the Payment

- Click **Accept Payment** to save the transaction.
- Click **Cancel** to exit without saving.

After you click **Accept Payment**:
* A confirmation message appears: 
		**"This transaction has been approved."**  
* Click **OK** to continue.

>⚠️ **Important:** Transactions will fail if the card is expired.

Once approved, the payment is recorded in the customer’s agreement history and reflected in their balance.

## Terminology Guide

| Term / Abbreviation    | Definition                                                                       |
| ---------------------- | -------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **SA**                 | Service Agreement. A contract for ongoing customer services.                     |
| **ACH**                | Automated Clearing House. An electronic network for bank-to-bank payments.       |
| **Transaction ID**     | A unique reference number for a payment (e.g., check number, electronic ID).     |
| **Agreement Details**  | Section in a Service Agreement record where actions (like payments) are managed. |
| **Installment Letter** | A letter that shows payment schedules for a Service Agreement.                   |
| **Renewal Proposal**   | A document to extend or renew a Service Agreement.                               |
