## Overview

Use the **Purchase Order (PO)** screen to create, edit, and manage purchase orders for suppliers.

You can:

- Create a new purchase order
    
- Add or remove line items
    
- Update pricing and quantities
    
- Track order totals
    
- Mark a purchase order as complete
    

---

## Before You Begin

Make sure you have:

- Supplier information
    
- Product IDs or item descriptions
    
- Quantity and pricing details
    
- Shipping and terms information
    

---

# Create a New Purchase Order

1.  Click **New PO**.
    
2. Verify that the system generates a **PO Number**.
    
3. Enter the **Date** if needed.
    

---

# Enter Supplier Information

1. In the **Supplier** section:
    
    - Select or enter the **Company name**.
        
    - Confirm the **Address**, **City/State/Zip**, and **Phone**.
        
2. Update any incorrect fields before continuing.
    

---

# Enter Customer Information 

1. In the **Customer** section:
    
    - Enter the **Ticket #** (if applicable).
        
    - Enter the **First Name** and **Last Name**.
        
    - Select a **PO Job Type**.
    
---

# Add Line Items

Use line items to add products or materials to the purchase order.

## Add a New Line

1. Click **New Line**.
    
2. Enter the **Product ID**.
    
3. Enter a **Description** (if not auto-filled).
    
4. Enter the **Quantity Ordered (QO)**.
    
5. Enter the **Price**.
    
6. Confirm the **Extended Amount** updates correctly.
    

Repeat these steps for each additional item.

---

## Remove a Line Item

1. Locate the item in the line item table.
    
2. Click the **red X** at the end of the row.
    
3. Confirm the deletion if prompted.
    

---

# Review Totals

The system automatically calculates:

- **Subtotal**
    
- **Discount**
    
- **Tax**
    
- **Shipping**
    
- **Total**
    

Review these values in the upper-right section of the screen before finalizing the order.

---

# Update Order Status

In the **Status** section:

1. Set **Terms** (for example, NET 30).
    
2. Enter **Requested By**.
    
3. Enter or confirm the **Ship Date**.
    
4. Enter shipping instructions in **Ship Via**.
    
5. Select **YES** or **NO** for **PO Complete**.
    

Select **YES** only after all items are received and verified.

---

# Print a Purchase Order

1. Click **Print PO** at the top of the screen.
    
2. Confirm printer settings.
    
3. Print or save as PDF.
    

---

# Find an Existing Purchase Order

To search for a PO:

1. Click **Find**.
    
2. Enter the PO Number or search criteria.
    
3. Click **Find All** to display all purchase orders.
    
4. Use the record navigation arrows to move between records.
    

You can also use **QuickFind Search** to search directly from the toolbar.

---

# Edit an Existing Purchase Order

1. Locate the PO using **Find** or navigation arrows.
    
2. Update the necessary fields.
    
3. Add or remove line items as needed.
    
4. Save changes according to your system configuration.
