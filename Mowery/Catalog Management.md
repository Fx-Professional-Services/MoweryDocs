# 📚 Catalog Management – Mowery

In Mowery, **catalogs are price books**. Each catalog contains a list of active products along with their associated prices and details. You can create multiple catalogs to support different pricing strategies, such as seasonal pricing, customer-specific pricing, or promotional offers.

This guide explains how to create, duplicate, and delete catalogs in the Mowery system.

---

## ➕ Create a Catalog

1. Navigate to **System Defaults → Catalog**.
2. Click the **New Catalog** button.
3. In the catalog window, enter the following details:
   - **Effective Date**
   - **Title**
   - **Revision**
   - Any other required fields
4. Click **Add Catalog**.
5. A dialog will appear confirming that catalog creation is in progress. Click **OK**.
6. When the process completes, another dialog will inform you whether the catalog creation succeeded or failed.
7. Click the catalog name to view its items.
8. Verify that the number of items matches the number of active products.

---

## 🌀 Duplicate a Catalog

1. Navigate to **System Defaults → Catalog**.
2. Select the catalog to duplicate by clicking the white area on the right side of the screen.
3. Click the **Duplicate Catalog** button.
4. In the duplication window, update the catalog information as needed.
5. Click **Duplicate Catalog** to begin the duplication process.
6. A dialog will appear indicating that duplication is in progress. Click **OK**.
7. After processing, a dialog will inform you whether the duplication succeeded or failed.
8. Click the duplicated catalog name to view its items.
9. Confirm that the number of items matches the original catalog.

---

## ❌ Delete a Catalog

1. Navigate to **System Defaults → Catalog**.
2. Select the catalog to delete by clicking the white area on the right side of the screen.
3. Click the **Delete Catalog** button in the upper-left corner.
4. A confirmation dialog will appear.
5. Click **Delete** to confirm or **No** to cancel the operation.
