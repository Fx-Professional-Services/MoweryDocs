# 📱 Connecting to the Mowery Service Call App (iPad Only)

  This guide explains how technicians can connect to the **Mowery Service Call system** using the **FileMaker Go** app on an **iPad**. Once connected, technicians can access customer information, service tickets, equipment records, and maintenance agreements.

---
## 👤 Who is this for?

  Field technicians using iPads to manage and complete service calls.

---
## ✅ Requirements

- An **iPad** with the [**FileMaker Go**](https://apps.apple.com/app/filemaker-go/id500553111) app installed.

- A valid **account name** and **password**.

- An active internet or cellular data connection.
---

## 🔌 How to Connect

### 1. Open FileMaker Go

  Open the **FileMaker Go** app on your iPad.

  > **Note:** If not installed, download it from the App Store.
---
### 2. Add the Host

  1. Tap **Hosts** on the FileMaker Go home screen.
  2. Tap the **+** icon to add a new host.
  3. Enter this host address: [fm2.mowefryservice.net](http://fm2.mowefryservice.net)
  4. Tap **Save**.
---
### 3. Open the Mowery App

1. Tap **mowerydb_mobile** under the saved host.
2. Sign in using your **account name** and **password**.
3. Tap **Sign In**.
---
## 🖥️ After Signing In

You will arrive at the **Service Call Dashboard**, which contains tabs for different sections of customer and job information.

---
## 📂 Tab Descriptions

<img src="https://github.com/Fx-Professional-Services/MoweryDocs/blob/c3824db36a51d0088498d5ca21e180ec3f0951ef/Mowery/service_call_tabs.png" width="400" height="300">

### 🔧 Service Setup

  This tab contains general customer information:

  - **Customer Name**
  - **Phone Number**
  - **Address**
  - **City, State, ZIP**
  - **Remarks** (e.g., EMPLOYEE)

Use this tab to confirm customer identity and job location.

---
### 📋 Ticket Details

  This tab shows all the key information for the current service ticket:

  - **Scheduled Date** – Date of the appointment.

- **Time Slot** – Assigned time window (e.g., 8–10 AM).

- **Service Department** – Team assigned (e.g., Heat Pump, Electrical).

- **Service Type** – Type of service (e.g., Repair, Maintenance).

- **Lead Source** – How the service call originated (e.g., Website, Referral).

- **Turn Over Tech** – Technician assigned to complete or revisit the job.

- **Attempt Again On** – Scheduled date for follow-up attempt if needed.

- **Call Taken By** – Dispatcher or staff member who created the call.

- **Payment Type** – Payment method selected (e.g., Credit, Cash, Invoice).

- **Description** – Free-text notes about the issue or customer request.

| Tip: Tap the Refresh button in the upper-left corner of the screen to reload the service details.

---

### 📜 Service Call History

  This tab displays a history of past service calls for the selected customer:

- Review work performed on previous visits

- Identify recurring issues

- Avoid duplicate efforts  

Use this tab to get a full picture of the customer’s service background.

---
### ⚙️ Equipment

  This tab lists all equipment at the customer’s service location:

  - **Model Numbers**

- **Serial Numbers**

- **Installation Dates**

- **Repair/Replacement History**

Use this to confirm you’re working on the correct unit and check past issues.

---
### 📆 Maintenance Agreements

This tab shows active or past service agreements tied to the customer:

- **Status** – Active, Expired, or Canceled

- **Expiration** – Agreement end date

- **Agreement Type** – Coverage plan (e.g., HVAC Annual, Plumbing Seasonal)

Use this tab to verify whether the customer’s work is covered under contract.

---
## 🛠 Troubleshooting

| Issue | Solution |

|-------|----------|

| Can't connect to host | Check Wi-Fi or cellular connection. Verify host is `fm2.mowefryservice.net`. |

| Login failed | Confirm you're using your **account name** (not email). Contact your dispatcher if needed. |

| Mowery Payment not showing | Re-check host entry or reinstall FileMaker Go. |

---
## 📌 Tips

- Add FileMaker Go to your home screen dock for easy access.

- Always review **Service Call History** and **Ticket Details** before visiting a job site.

- Check the **Equipment** and **Maintenance Agreement** tabs to confirm coverage and work requirements.

- Keep your iPad charged and software updated to avoid issues during the day.

---

For questions or login help, contact your dispatcher or service manager.
