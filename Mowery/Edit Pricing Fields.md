## ✏️ Edit Catalog Item Pricing

You can modify the pricing fields of individual items within a catalog to reflect updated rates or market-specific pricing.

### Steps

1. Go to **System Defaults → Catalog**.
2. Double-click the **title** of the catalog you want to open. The list of catalog items appears.
3. Select a catalog item from the list.
4. Click the **leftmost button** next to the **Task Code** to open the item details.
5. Edit any of the following pricing fields as needed:
   - **Standard Rate** – The base price used for regular transactions.
   - **Value Rate** – A rate based on value-driven pricing models.
   - **Commercial Rate** – A special rate for commercial or bulk purchases.
6. When a field is modified, a **reset icon** appears. Click it to revert the value to the original system-generated rate.
7. Click **Done** when you finish editing.

> **Note**: Item-level pricing overrides the catalog defaults and is saved individually for each catalog item.

## 🧾 Edit Catalog Tax Rate

You can set or update the default tax rate for an entire catalog in the **Catalog Price Settings** section.

### Steps

1. Go to **System Defaults → Catalog**.
2. Select a catalog from the list.
3. Click the **leftmost button** next to the **Code** column to open the catalog details.
4. In the **Catalog Price Settings** section, find the **Tax** field.
5. Enter a new tax rate (e.g., `0%` for tax-exempt catalogs).
6. Click **Update Price** to apply the changes.

> **Tip**: This tax rate is applied to all catalog items by default. You can override it at the item level if needed.
