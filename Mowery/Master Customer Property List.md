
## 🧾 Master Customer Property List

The **Master Customer Property List** (also called the **Customer Property Card List**) provides a searchable table of all customers in the system. It allows you to quickly view, sort, and manage customer property records.

This layout is used frequently by service staff, dispatchers, and office administrators to view contact info, service agreements, phone numbers, and equipment counts for each property.

---

### 🔍 What You Can Do

- Search for a customer by name, phone number, or address  
- View key details like service status, phone numbers, equipment, and service agreements  
- Click the blue **DETAIL VIEW** arrow to open the full customer profile  
- Use sorting or filtering to manage large customer lists  
- See whether a property has any open service agreements or parts orders  
- Print or review account details for scheduling, billing, or dispatch  

---

### 📋 Columns and Their Purpose

| Column | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| **Customer Type** | Indicates whether the property is **Residential** or **Commercial**. |
| **Customer / Email** | Displays the customer name and email address. |
| **Bill To Property / City / ZIP** | Shows the billing address for the customer. |
| **Phone Numbers** | Lists all phone numbers associated with the account. |
| **Phone Descriptions** | Identifies the owner of each phone (e.g., HUSBAND, WIFE, JESSICA). |
| **# SAs** | Number of **Service Agreements** linked to the property. |
| **# PAs** | Number of **Proposals** created for the customer. |
| **# Equip.** | Number of equipment items listed for the property. |
| **Service Agreement Status** | Displays whether the customer’s service agreement is **ACTIVE**, **EXPIRED**, or blank (no agreement). |

---

### ⚙️ Additional Tools

At the top of the layout, you can access:

- **New Customer** – Create a new customer record  
- **Find** / **Find All** – Search or view all records  
- **Sort** – Organize records by city, ZIP code, or name  
- **QuickFind Search** – Instantly search by phone number (area code required)  

---

### ➡️ Detail View

Click the blue **arrow button** on the left side of a customer row to open the **Detail View**.  
Here, you can view and edit:

- Full address and contact info  
- Linked equipment records  
- Active or expired service agreements  
- Service call history  
- Communication logs and notes  

---

### 📝 Tips

- If multiple phone numbers are listed, the **Phone Descriptions** field tells you who each number belongs to.  
- Use the **Sort** button to group customers by city or ZIP for routing or bulk scheduling.  
- You can see both **Bill To** and **Property** address lines—these may differ for landlords or rental properties.  

---

### 🔐 Access Notes

Only authorized users can edit or delete customer records. If a record is locked or grayed out, contact your supervisor.
