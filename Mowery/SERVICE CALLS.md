## 🖨️ Print to Parts from a Service Call

  You can generate a printed parts list directly from a Service Call record using the **Print to Parts** feature.

### Steps

1. From the main menu, go to **Service Call**.

2. In the leftmost column (highlighted in fluorescent blue), click a service card. This opens the customer property or service call data entry screen.

3. Scroll to the **bottom right** of the page (Step 4 section).

4. Click the **Print to Parts** button.

   - A print layout will appear.

   - If you see a **"Printer not found"** error, you can ignore it. This usually means a printer is not connected to your network.

5. After clicking the button, the **Printed to Parts** field (in the bottom left) updates with:

   - The **account name** of the user who clicked the button.

   - The **timestamp** of when the print action occurred.

> **Note**: This field acts as a log, tracking who initiated the print and when.

---
### 🛠️ Default Printer Behavior

When you click the **Print to Parts** button, the system automatically prints to the **default parts printer**.
### Benefits
- No need to manually select a printer each time.

- Reduces errors from incorrect printer selection.

- Improves speed and efficiency during dispatch operations.

## 📘 Definitions

  ### **Service Call**

A *Service Call* refers to a scheduled or on-demand visit by a technician or service provider to a customer's location to perform maintenance, repair, installation, or inspection work. It includes key details such as customer information, service address, work tasks, assigned personnel, and materials used.
### **Print to Parts**

The *Print to Parts* feature allows dispatchers or service technicians to generate and print a parts list directly from a service call record. This list typically includes all the parts required or used during the service visit.
### **Default Parts Printer**

A *Default Parts Printer* is a pre-configured printer specifically designated for printing parts lists. When the **Print to Parts** button is pressed, the system automatically sends the print job to this printer without requiring the user to select a printer manually.
