# 🌐 Technician Profile Page

This page explains what customers see when they click the **Technician Profile** link sent via text message from the Service Call system.

---
## 📋 Overview

Customers receive a secure link to a **Technician Profile Page** in their service notification text. This page provides helpful details about the upcoming appointment and the assigned technician(s).

---
## 🔧 What the Page Includes

### 🛠️ Service Information

- **Status** – Indicates whether the job is Scheduled, In Progress, or Completed.

- **Appointment Time** – The scheduled date and time of the technician's arrival.

- **Service Description** – A brief summary of the work to be performed.

- **Service Type** – The category of service (e.g., Plumbing, HVAC, Electrical).

### 👷 Technician Information

- **Name(s)** – The names of the technician(s) assigned to your service.

- **Photo(s)** – A photo of each technician so you know who to expect.

- **Bio (if available)** – A short introduction to help you get to know your technician.  

---
## 🔒 Security and Accessibility

- Each page has a **unique, secure link** tied to the specific service call and technician.

- Customers **cannot browse or search** for other technicians.

- No login is required—the page opens directly from the link in the message.

- The page is fully **mobile-friendly** and works on any device.
