
### 🌐 What the Technician Profile Page Shows

When customers click the link in the message, they’ll be taken to a secure webpage with helpful information about their upcoming service.

#### 🔧 Service Information

- **Status** – Current status of the service call (e.g., Scheduled, In Progress, Completed)

- **Appointment Time** – The date and time the technician is expected to arrive

- **Service Description** – A brief explanation of the work to be performed

- **Service Type** – The category of service (e.g., Plumbing, HVAC, Electrical)

#### 👷 Technician Information

- **Names** – The names of all technicians assigned to the service call

- **Photos** – Pictures of each technician, so the customer knows who to expect
