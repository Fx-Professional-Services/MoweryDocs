# 🕘 View Product Price History

This article explains how to view historical pricing data for products in the Mowery FileMaker system.

---
## 📋 Overview

The system maintains a record of all pricing changes for each product. This information supports auditing, tracking, and understanding how pricing evolves over time.

---
## 🔍 How to View Price History

1. Go to **System Defaults → Products**.

2. Click the **leftmost button** next to the product name to open its detail view.

3. In the upper-right corner, locate the **Price History Tracking** panel.

---
## 📄 What the Panel Displays

The **Price History Tracking** panel shows a list of pricing updates. Each entry includes the following fields:

- **Effective Date** – The date when the price change became active.

- **Revision** – The version number or identifier of the update.

- **Standard Rate** – The default price for regular sales.

- **Value Rate** – A rate used for value-based pricing.

- **Addon Standard** – Add-on price under the standard rate.

- **Addon Value** – Add-on price under the value rate.

- **Commercial** – A rate applied to commercial or bulk transactions.

---

> ℹ️ All changes to pricing fields are automatically recorded in this panel for auditability and reference.
