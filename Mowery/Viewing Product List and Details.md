
# 📦 View Product List and Details

  Follow these steps to view the product list and details:

1. From the menu, go to **System Defaults → Products**. This will display the list of products.

2. To view the details of a specific product, locate the product in the list and click on it.

3. On the product detail page, you can view the following information:

   - **Product Info**: General details about the product.

   - **Product Price Attributes**: Pricing-related details for the product.

   - **Current Pricing**: The product's price based on the current effective date or any earlier dates.

   - **Pricing Tracking and History Portal**: This section, located on the upper right, shows the product’s pricing changes over time.

  

> **Note**: In the developer settings, you can configure the system to auto-enter add-on and commercial rates when creating a new product by clicking the **New Product** button.
