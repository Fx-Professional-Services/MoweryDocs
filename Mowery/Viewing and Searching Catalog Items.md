
# 📚 Viewing and Searching Catalog Items

## View a Catalog

1. Go to **System Defaults → Catalog**.
2. Select a catalog from the list and click on it to open.
3. Click the **leftmost button** next to the **Code** of the catalog to open its details.
4. Once the catalog is open, click **View Catalog** to display the list of catalog items.
5. You can now modify pricing fields, tax rates, and other catalog details as needed.

---

## Search Catalog Items

1. Go to **System Defaults → Catalog**.
2. Select a catalog from the list.
3. In the **Catalog Items** section, locate the **search bar** at the top of the catalog list.
4. Enter a keyword, product code, or phrase related to the catalog item you're looking for.
5. Press **Enter** or click the search icon.
   - If no matching results are found, a message will appear indicating **no results found**, but the list of catalog items will remain visible.
